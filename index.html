No added preservatives. All natural honey.

We are Kamloops organic honey provider

Bootstrap Apiaries was founded by Leroy in 2014. Leroy is interested in sustainability of ecosystems and the economy.

“Bootstrap” was chosen as a name as Leroy has built everything from scratch, recycling waste wood and starting new colonies. It is a model that while inherent in natural systems (no such thing as unemployment in these), but is getting increasingly scarce in modern economic systems.

Leroy's education background is in plant and insect ecology with related interests in evolution and system dynamics.
